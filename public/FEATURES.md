# 🚀 Features – Early Prediction of Student Stress System

## 🔮 1. Early Stress Prediction

* Predicts **future stress levels (next day)**
* Helps in **early intervention before stress becomes severe**

---

## 📊 2. Behavioral Data Analysis

* Uses real-world student behavior:

  * Phone usage 📱
  * Sleep patterns 😴
  * Physical activity 🏃
  * Location & movement 📍

---

## 🧠 3. Machine Learning Models

* LSTM (Time-series prediction)
* Random Forest
* XGBoost

---

## 🔍 4. Explainable AI (XAI)

* Uses:

  * SHAP
  * LIME
* Provides clear reasons for predictions

📌 Example:

* Low sleep → High stress contribution
* High phone usage → Medium contribution

---

## 🚨 5. Early Warning System

* Alerts students before stress becomes high
* Helps in taking preventive actions

---

## 📈 6. Stress Level Classification

* Low
* Medium
* High

---

## 📊 7. Visualization Dashboard (Optional)

* Graphs for:

  * Sleep vs Stress
  * Phone usage vs Stress
* Easy understanding of patterns

---

## ⚡ 8. Unique Highlights

* ✅ Predicts stress BEFORE it occurs
* ✅ Provides explanation (not black-box)
* ✅ Uses practical behavioral data

---

## 🔧 9. Scalable Design

* Can be extended to:

  * Mobile applications
  * Real-time monitoring systems
  * Wearable device integration
